Crisis & support lines

National Domestic Violence Hotline

Call 1-800-799-7233 or text “START” to 88788 for confidential support related to abuse.

988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline

Call or text 988 for free, confidential support, 24/7, if you or someone you know is in crisis.

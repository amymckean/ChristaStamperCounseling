About Christa

More than 30 years of ministry

Christa Stamper is a biblical counselor with a particular heart for those wounded by abuse, oppression, relational betrayal, and life’s deepest trials. Her approach combines theological depth with compassionate care — helping people understand their stories through the grand narrative of Scripture: creation, fall, redemption, and restoration.

Her training includes a Master of Arts in Counseling from Westminster Theological Seminary, Formation and Essentials certificates from CCEF, and a Victim Advocacy and Trauma-Informed Care Certificate from Give Her Wings Academy.

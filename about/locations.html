Available virtually

Christa offers counseling sessions virtually, by secure video call. This means you can meet with her from wherever you are — home, a quiet office, or anywhere you have privacy and a reliable internet connection — without needing to travel.

In-person sessions are not currently available.

Christa is based in the Seattle area, WA, and is able to work with clients located anywhere, as long as virtual counseling is a good fit for your needs.

If you're unsure whether virtual counseling will work for your situation, feel free to reach out with questions before scheduling.

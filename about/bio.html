Christa Stamper

MA, Counselor

Christa Stamper is a biblical counselor with more than 30 years of ministry experience walking alongside women, families, and communities through seasons of suffering, struggle, and transformation. She has led women’s Bible studies and support groups, counseled women and youth, developed mentoring ministries, and served through speaking and writing.

Christa has a particular heart for those wounded by abuse, oppression, relational betrayal, and life’s deepest trials. She previously served as Program Manager at a Christian domestic violence shelter, where she provided compassionate support, biblical encouragement, and practical care to women and families seeking safety, healing, and restoration.

Her approach to biblical counseling combines theological depth with compassionate care. She seeks to understand the full complexity of each person’s story while helping them view their struggles through the grand narrative of Scripture — creation, fall, redemption, and restoration. Christa believes the heart of God revealed in Christ brings genuine hope and healing to those who suffer.

Her ministry is shaped by decades of walking with people through hardship, along with formal training in biblical counseling, theology, and trauma-informed care. She brings a deep love for Scripture, a passion for the oppressed and hurting, and a desire to equip individuals, churches, and communities to respond to suffering with wisdom, truth, and compassion. Christa also partners with church leadership to assess complex situations and develop biblically grounded care plans.

Christa lives with her husband, Greg. They recently celebrated their 32nd wedding anniversary and are enjoying life as new empty nesters. They have four young adult children, two of whom are married, and recently welcomed their first grandchild.

Training & Credentials

  • Master of Arts in Counseling, Westminster Theological Seminary, Philadelphia, PA
  • Formation Certificate and Essentials of Biblical Counseling Certificate, Christian Counseling & Educational Foundation
  • Victim Advocacy and Trauma-Informed Care Certificate, Give Her Wings Academy

Christa is not a licensed clinical therapist, but a trained biblical counselor. Her sessions are not a substitute for licensed mental health treatment. If you are in crisis, please see the resources in the footer below.

Christa Stamper

MA, Counselor

Christa Stamper is a biblical counselor who helps people find hope, wisdom, and lasting comfort in Christ through the truth of God’s Word. With more than thirty years of ministry experience, she has walked alongside women, families, and church communities through seasons of suffering, trauma, betrayal, abuse, and transformation.

Christa’s compassion for the hurting was deeply shaped by growing up with her older sister Tara, who sustained a traumatic brain injury as a young child and lived with significant disabilities. Through Tara’s life, Christa learned to see the dignity and worth of every person, especially the vulnerable and overlooked. This compassion continues to shape her ministry today.

Throughout her years of ministry, Christa has led women’s Bible studies and support groups, counseled women and youth, developed mentoring ministries, and served through speaking, writing, and leadership. She has a particular heart for those wounded by abuse, oppression, relational betrayal, and life’s deepest trials. She previously served as Program Manager at a Christian domestic violence shelter, where she provided leadership, biblical encouragement, trauma-informed care, advocacy, and practical support to women and families in crisis.

Like those she serves, Christa has personally known seasons of weakness and deep dependence on God’s grace. Her counseling flows not only from extensive training and experience, but from tasting the kindness and faithfulness of the Lord who sustains His people in hardship.

Christa’s approach combines theological depth with compassionate care. She seeks to understand the full complexity of each person’s story while helping them view their struggles within the grand narrative of Scripture — creation, fall, redemption, and restoration. Rather than offering simplistic answers, she points people to the hope and steadfast love found in Christ.

Christa and her husband Greg have been married thirty-two years and live in Washington State. They have four adult children, two of whom are married, and recently welcomed their first grandchild.

Training & Credentials

  • Master of Arts in Counseling, Westminster Theological Seminary, Philadelphia, PA
  • Formation Certificate and Essentials of Biblical Counseling Certificate, Christian Counseling & Educational Foundation
  • Victim Advocacy and Trauma-Informed Care Certificate, Give Her Wings Academy

Christa is not a licensed clinical therapist, but a trained biblical counselor. Her sessions are not a substitute for licensed mental health treatment. If you are in crisis, please see the resources in the footer below.
